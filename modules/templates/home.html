What is FlightsDetecor?

Flight Detector is created for managing flights of different directions and finding the most beneficial in terms of money and time saving ones. The results are presented in user-friendly web-applications(one of them performs alternative flights analysis and the other one - route analysis), which allow to get flights and present them in a shape of graphs and web-maps by indicating the required parameters.
