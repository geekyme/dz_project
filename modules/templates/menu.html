Route analysis

This is route analysis app. By opening this app you will be able:
to see the web-map portraying your route;
to explore the table(sorted by price), which will present data about tickets;
to see the graph visualizing how the prices fluctuate depending on filter type;
to choose the desired price range, by moving the slider of prices;

Alternative flights analysis

This is alternative flights analysis app. By opening this app you will be able: to see the web-map proposing all alternative directions to the entered destination city;
to get those alternative flights on the bubbled graph(the OY axis will show the distance from origin city to alternative city) and each bubble represents an alternative flight;
by hovering on the wanted bubble toget another graph, which presents additional information about that flight tickets prices;
